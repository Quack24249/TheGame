Our Idea

Our idea for this game was to create a game that people could play in their down time. We wanted to flex that creatice muscle and see what we could do.

The Story of Creation

We are just three high schoolers who decided it would be fun to make a game. So we got together and did just that.

What We Used

We used GitHub to store our game file, Google Docs to help with organization, and Unreal Engine 4 to make the actual game.
